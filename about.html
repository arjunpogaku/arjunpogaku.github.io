Biography

Arjun Chakravarthi Pogaku, born in 1997 in Madanapalle, Andhra Pradesh, India, is a dedicated researcher working at the intersection of Pattern Mining, Data Science, large language models (LLMs), and Wireless Communications. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. in the Graduate Department of Computer and Information Systems at the University of Aizu, Japan, under the supervision of Dr. Uday Kiran RAGE.
